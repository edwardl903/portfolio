Research

Overview

This research project focused on analyzing electroencephalogram (EEG) data to understand human cognitive processes, particularly in the context of strategic thinking and decision-making. The study involved collecting and analyzing neural activity data from over 20 participants to identify patterns in brain activity during complex cognitive tasks.

Research Objectives

  • Neural Pattern Analysis: Identify brain activity patterns during strategic thinking tasks
  • Cognitive Process Mapping: Map neural responses to different types of decision-making scenarios
  • Statistical Validation: Apply rigorous statistical methods to validate findings
  • Data Visualization: Create comprehensive visualizations of neural activity patterns
  • Research Contribution: Contribute to the understanding of human cognition and strategic thinking

Methodology

Data Collection

EEG recordings from 20+ participants during strategic thinking tasks

Data Processing

Signal processing and noise reduction using Python and R

Statistical Analysis

Advanced statistical methods for pattern recognition and validation

Visualization

Comprehensive data visualization for research presentation

Technical Implementation

  • EEG Data Processing: Signal filtering, artifact removal, and feature extraction
  • Statistical Analysis: Advanced statistical methods including time-frequency analysis
  • Data Visualization: Creation of informative charts and graphs for research presentation
  • Research Documentation: Comprehensive documentation of methods and findings
  • Quality Assurance: Rigorous validation of data processing and analysis methods

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Complex EEG Data

Processing and analyzing complex EEG signals with multiple channels and high noise levels.

Solution: Implemented advanced signal processing techniques and noise reduction algorithms to extract meaningful neural patterns.

Challenge: Statistical Validation

Ensuring statistical rigor and validity of findings across multiple participants and conditions.

Solution: Applied robust statistical methods and conducted thorough validation to ensure reliable and reproducible results.
